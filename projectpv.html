1. Executive Summary

Objective: The primary objective of this project is to develop and deploy a comprehensive fitness center web application for Raptors Fitness Center. This application will feature a chatbot-enabled onboarding process that guides users through a personalized questionnaire to analyze their fitness goals and preferences. The application will offer user account functionality, secure data storage, and AI-enabled modules to provide tailored fitness recommendations and guidance.

Corporate Goals Addressed: Customer Satisfaction, Revenue Growth, Efficiency and Effectiveness, Data-Driven Decision-Making, Compliance and Security.

Planned Start Date: Sep 25, 2023

Planned End Date: Friday, March 29, 2024

3. Scope

In Scope: Development and implementation of the fitness center web app, Design and deployment of comprehensive questionnaires, Development and integration of AI analysis modules.

Out of Scope: Physical gym equipment or facilities, Marketing and advertising campaigns, Mobile app development, Non-fitness related services or products.

4. Deliverables

Deliverable: Fitness center web app with AI-enabled chatbot.

This deliverable encompasses the development and deployment of a comprehensive fitness center web application. The web app is equipped with an AI-enabled chatbot that serves as a virtual fitness assistant.

Deliverable: Comprehensive questionnaires.

This deliverable includes the creation and implementation of user-friendly and comprehensive questionnaires within the web application. These questionnaires are designed to gather essential information from users about their fitness goals, preferences, and health conditions.

Deliverable: AI analysis modules.

The AI Analysis Modules seamlessly integrate with the Botpress chatbot to enhance the user experience. These modules leverage advanced natural language processing (NLP) and conversation analysis techniques.

5. Assumptions

This project makes the following assumptions:

  • Users will have internet access.
  • Users will provide accurate information in the questionnaires.
  • Data storage will comply with relevant regulations.
